OUR PROGRESS

The product has currently been developed as a proper Web Application available at MSAI.ML . Due to the constraints of evaluating the accuracy of the ml used in our system, we used our same implementation for another set of data which is used to predict diabetics type 2 in children and young adults and found the accuracy to be approx. ~84%. To convert the product from an MVP to a quality product, we need to acquire data for all the different courses and colleges available and their scores and SOP’s. What we require for the first year is to accumulate the necessary data and to publish this product as a Webapp or an android/iOS application.
